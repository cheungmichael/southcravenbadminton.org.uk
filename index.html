Sutton and South Craven Badminton Club

Formed in 1982, the Sutton and South Craven Badminton Club meets every Tuesday from 7.30pm - 9.00pm in the Main Sports Hall at South Craven School, Cross Hills.

We are a non-league club and anyone of all abilities is welcome to come along. The cost for each session is £5.00.
